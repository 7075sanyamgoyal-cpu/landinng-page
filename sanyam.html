Why communities choose P.E.A

Built with learners, teachers and families at the centre.

Free access

Curated learning materials aligned to school ages 6–18, with printable options.

Teacher tools

Adaptable resources, lesson outlines and simple progress tracking.

Inclusive by design

Supports learners with disabilities, attentional and language needs.

For families

Affordable pathways to support learning at home and after school.

Community & partners

NGO and school partnerships to reach rural and regional learners.

Simple & scalable

Lightweight, works on low-connectivity devices with offline packs (future).

What’s inside

  • • Age‑banded learning modules (6–18) with adaptable difficulty
  • • Printable lesson plans and teacher notes
  • • Accessibility features and multilingual support (roadmap)
  • • Simple learner progress view for teachers and parents
  • • Community hub for sharing tips and success stories
